A Community Land Trust In Mill Valley

The Mt Tam Community Land Trust is a local non-profit dedicated to creating, preserving and sustaining long term affordable housing in Mill Valley.

Through outreach, tenant advocacy and property management, we strive to support a thriving, vibrant and diverse community.

We love this town, but

We have a housing crisis. Working people cannot afford to buy—or even rent—local housing. Ninety percent of the people who work in our town commute in.

How We’re Fixing It

As a community land trust, Mt Tam CLT takes land and housing out of the speculative real estate market and holds it “in trust” to serve as a long-term community asset for housing people.

Affordable Housing

Providing services and housing for both long time residents and those new to town.

Community Enrichment

Bringing the community together through events, community meetings, education and opportunities for engagement.

Sustainable Stewardship for the Future

Improving Mill Valley through community projects and local initiatives.

What is a Community Land Trust?

Community land trusts (CLTs) are set up and run by ordinary people to develop and manage homes as well as other assets important to that community, like community enterprises, food growing or workspaces. CLTs act as long-term stewards of housing, ensuring that it remains genuinely affordable, based on what people actually earn in their area, not just for now but for every future occupier.
